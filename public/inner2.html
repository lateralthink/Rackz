Rackz Gallery X Gerard Riera

Pandora

The lights go on! After more than two years of recording and work, the Rackz Gallery team shows the world its first piece of art in the form of a video. A 15-minute-long piece where the style and aesthetics of Skateboarding prevails and where the most prominent and promising riders of the national scene transmit the philosophy and principles of what the team understands as Skateboarding.

To commemorate this long-awaited launch and immortalize the best moments of this journey, the company has counted with the collaboration of Gerard Riera, one of the best photographers in the world and who has a countless number of publications in magazines such as Trasher, FREE, SOLO and Dogway.

Gerard and the team share a very clear philosophy in this world and that is that Skateboarding is art and for that reason they´ve joined forces to launch the first project with NFT's where both offer the possibility to defenders of the same philosophy, to be a part of the project by acquiring these unique works of art. Many of the works have been published in magazines and now the team give the possibility to individuals to be the sole owners of these pieces. Anticipate the future and join the movement.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for 'non-fungible token. An NFT is basically data that is accounted for in a digital ledger, and that data represents something specific and unique. An NFT can, for example, represent a unique piece of art or a game token.

What does “mint” mean?

NFT Minting is the process by which your digital art or digital content becomes a part of the blockchain.

How to Mint a NFT?

Conect you Metamask Wallet. To get Started you will first need to open a crypto wallet and then connect it to our Virtual Gallery.

How to create a MetaMask Wallet

we recommend you to visit the official website https://metamask.io/. For more information you can contact our technical team at support@rackzgallery.com.

How much will it cost?

The cost is 350 MATIC. To this cost must be added the cost of Gas charged by the network, which does not depend on us. We recommend that you find out the times with low network fees in your place of residence.

How many NFT can I buy?

There is no limit on purchases per registered Wallet.

WHICH BLOCKCHAIN NETWORK ARE WE WORKING ON?

We will work on the Polygon network, using its MATIC token (https://www.polygon.com/). This network is a secure and innovative network with one of the lowest commissions in the market.

HOW CAN I BUY

We recommend you to buy your cryptocurrencies through your Metamask App or trusted exchange.
